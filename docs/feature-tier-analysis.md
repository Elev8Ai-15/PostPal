## Tier Improvement Recommendations

### 1. Clarify "Campaign Research" vs "In-depth Campaign Research"
**Issue**: The distinction is unclear.
**Recommendation**: 
- **Campaign Research** (Free/Basic) = AI-generated content ideas, basic topic suggestions
- **In-depth Campaign Research** (Pro/Vibe) = Full strategy generation, competitor analysis, content pillars, 12-month timeline

### 2. Add Missing High-Value Features to Tiers

| Feature | Recommended Tier |
|---------|------------------|
| AI Hashtag Suggestions | Free (drives engagement) |
| Content Calendar | Free (core functionality) |
| Push Notifications | Free (improves retention) |
| Recurring Templates | Basic+ (automation value) |
| Unified Social Inbox | Basic+ (engagement tool) |
| Saved Replies | Basic+ (efficiency) |
| Auto-Responders | Pro+ (automation premium) |
| AI Strategy Generation | Pro+ (high value) |
| Campaign Management | Pro+ (multi-platform) |
| AI Subreddit Targeting | Pro+ (Reddit-specific) |

### 3. Reconsider Post Limits

**Current Issue**: Pro tier has same 4 posts/week as Basic, which doesn't justify the price jump.

**Recommendation**:
- Free: 1 post/week
- Basic: 10 posts/week (more reasonable for $4.99)
- Pro: 30 posts/week (power users)
- Vibe: Unlimited

### 4. Add Team/Collaboration Features to Vibe

**Missing from Vibe**: No team features despite being the premium tier.

**Recommendation for Vibe**:
- Multiple team members
- Role-based permissions
- Approval workflows
- White-label options
- Priority support response time (24hr)
- Dedicated account manager (optional add-on)

### 5. Consider Annual Pricing Discounts

**Recommendation**:
- Basic: $4.99/mo or $49.99/yr (17% savings)
- Pro: $9.99/mo or $99.99/yr (17% savings)
- Vibe: $19.99/mo or $179.99/yr (25% savings)

## Revenue Optimization Notes

1. **Free tier is generous** - Consider it a lead generation tool, not revenue
2. **Basic is the volume tier** - Price it to convert free users
3. **Pro is the profit tier** - Most features, best margin
4. **Vibe is the enterprise tier** - Premium pricing for premium needs

**Estimated conversion funnel**:
- Free → Basic: 10-15% conversion
- Basic → Pro: 20-30% conversion
- Pro → Vibe: 5-10% conversion
